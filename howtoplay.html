What's Sparrowz app?


Sparrowz app allows gamification of events through scavenger hunts.

Get started with Sparrowz

If you are interested to give it a try, then do the following:

  • 1Download our Sparrowz app
  • 2Choose a game you like
  • 3Play the scavenger hunt & discover more
Start now

What you can do with Sparrowz ?

  • Local Area Discovery – Find hunts near you and discover the hidden treasures around you.
  • Venue Discovery – Can be organized at a known or unknown venue like Library, Museums, Office buildings, Residences, Party Area, Resorts, Hotels, etc. for a discovery/challenge experience.
  • Outdoor / Indoor events for team building.
  • Induction/Orientation/On boarding programs for new hires/students at office/colleges/schools.
  • Building curated travel programs for your guests.
  • Personal events for a get together or festival celebrations.
  • Conferences – Get participants involved and visit every vendor in a exciting way and offer rewards.
  • Brand/Product launch.
  • Multi activity Quiz against the conventional Q&A types.
  • Create treasure hunts and Challenge your friends.
  • And Many More…

How to Play

  • Login / Register

    • Register for a new account or login with an existing account in Sparrowz. You can also register with your facebook account.

  • Select a game you like

    • For your city, there will be a list of games in "Browse Hunt" section. Select a game you like. If your city is not listed, select a city from the option given to you and play the game.

  • Play & Discover

    • Play the game and discover new places for yourself. May be, you may get something new to learn.

  • Share your experience

    • In the gameplay end, you are asked to give feedback along with response. Kindly, share your experience with us.

  • GloryBoard

    • After the game, your score along with your position in glory board of that game is shown to you. You can check the same in "GloryBoard" section.
