Think you know Cubbon Park ?

We Challenge your confidence!

Our Friends who KNOW Cubbon, have created a Scavenger Hunt to test your spatial awareness. The game routes are specially curated to take you to all the best corners of the 'Heart of Bangalore' (now, Lalbagh we're coming soon).

The clues could be simple or tough.. its just a state of mind after all. Solve the clues, find the place, validate , Repeat and demonstrate mastery. Soak in the fresh air, after all the winner is not the fastest, but the most aware!

Our treasure hunts are built as a game for all ages. Play in teams of 2 - 4
  -Come along with a friend, play against your friends
  -Bring your family, and do a parents vs. kids challenge...
These sub plots to our larger plot is the reason we have curated this game for Bangalore.



Oh Yes, this is probably the second time ever an app is being used for a Treasure Hunt in Bangalore (No Prizes for any guess on when an app was used for a treasure hunt for the first time :D)

Not only are we giving to you the 'funnest' way ever to discover Bangalore, but also are we giving away rewards, and / or Memorabilia.

FAQ

What is the event?

A treasure hunt in Cubbon Park, Solve the clues, find the location, perform action, get next clue.. Repeat

What do I need to play the game?

Smartphone with 3 hours battery life, LOADS of enthusiasm to search and Find or ask and find. Afterall the total distance you need to walk is < 3.5 kms over the time it takes you to find the clues.

Does the fastest team win the game?

Very unlikely, the team that takes the best images, that finds all the clues and plays fair is the team that will be declared as our Cubbon Champion of the week.

Why Smartphone?

The treasure hunt clues are given on a Mobile App - Sparrowz, Game password will be mailed to everyone who registers for the event. Registrations are possible on Events High.

Oh, How much of my data plan will be consumed while playing the game?

You only need data to download the app and the game (on wifi, well before you come to cubbon). Then the app consumes almost no data during game play. You however will only know your leaderboard position when you have internet.

Is the registration fee a fee of INR 300 per person?

No you only pay INR 300 PER TEAM. One Team can have upto 4 people playing. We recommend ideal team sizes of 2 or 3 people.

Can we play Alone?

Of course Yes, but registration fee is still INR 300. A team always makes it great fun to solve the challenges.

Spot Registrations?

Yes, But only subject to availability of slots.

What do we need to collect before the start of the game?

In person nothing! Your game passwords - we will email to you, and our team will be there on the ground to assist you, for anything else that we haven't thought of here.

What is the Treasure?

Your smiles!
Also, everyone's a winner with our personalised memorabilia

Why should i do this on a Sunday Morning?

Great way to spend time with Family & Friends...
Cubbon has no traffic!
Suitable for age group from 6 - 60
